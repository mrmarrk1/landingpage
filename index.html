Volunteer Maine

Volunteered to assist with setting up the venue and supporting the awardees at the Governor's Awards for Service and Volunteerism in Augusta. I had the opportunity to meet the Commissioner of the Maine state Department of Education and SVP Chief Marketing Officer of Skowhegan Savings Bank. It's an honor to contribute to an event that recognizes the invaluable contributions of volunteers across Maine.

Service Project

Thomas College Dirty Dog Mud Run. Assisted participants throughout the course, ensuring their safety and offering encouragement as they tackled each obstacle. Cleaning up event grounds afterward adds to the satisfaction of helping others push their limits and reach their goals in this thrilling event.

Thomas College Cup

As a volunteer helping with coordination for The Thomas Cup, I facilitated participants' access to a range of workshops, from cyber defense to crime scene analysis. We welcomed individuals of all skill levels and provided extensive support to these students. This opportunity not only allows me to serve the community but also empowers students to pursue STEM education while competing. Additionally, I dedicated myself to the cause, staying overnight to ensure the smooth running of the competition by keeping track of scores.

Thomas College Marketing Team

It was a rewarding opportunity to contribute to capturing and sharing memorable moments.

Time at Virtual Career Office

Volunteered as a content creation team member at a virtual career office, responsible for producing pre-event, event, and post-event content using Adobe Creative Cloud. With expertise in DSLR camera operation, I captured and documented activities over two years.
